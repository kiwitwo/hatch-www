About Us
Hatch is a third-party project hosting service for the Scratch programming language. Scratch is a great option to learn the fundamentals of programming, but it is designed for young children. Many Scratchers understandably find themselves outgrowing Scratch as they get older, and/or have distaste for new Scratch features, but they want to keep using the Scratch language. Hatch was designed with this group of users in mind. While we're very similar to Scratch, having a close online community, we have more relaxed rules.
Frequently Asked Questions
Who can join Hatch?
We allow anyone who is aged 13 or older to join. If you are under 13, you are welcome to use Hatch while logged out and access any non-13+ projects.
Why should I use Hatch?
There are other good third-party options for hosting Scratch projects. However, Hatch is far less limited. We are very transparent between the Hatch Team and Hatchlings (users), and our rules are much more relaxed. We also contain features that some other similar sites may not, such as commenting.
Why don't you have an editor?
We haven't implemented an editor yet because we didn't see a huge need to add one immediately. Instead, you can make a project in the official Scratch Editor or the third-party TurboWarp Editor, then upload the *.sb3 file to Hatch.
Can I advertise Hatch or my Hatch Projects on Scratch?
No. Scratch has rules that prohibit sharing links to websites with looser moderation guidelines, because Scratch is a platform for users of all ages. Please do not share links to Hatch or mention Hatch anywhere on the Scratch website.
There's a ratings system? How does that work?
Yeah. When you upload a project to Hatch, moderators will review your project and determine what age group the project is appropriate for. There are four ratings:
E: Suitable for all ages. Comparable to what you would find on Scratch.
7+: Not 9+-level inappropriate, but would be marked as NFE on Scratch.
9+: Projects at this rating would be taken down if they were uploaded to Scratch. May contain anything from the previous ratings, as well as some violence, mild cartoonish blood, light profanity, personal information, and intense scares.
13+: May contain anything from the previous ratings, as well as profanity usage, intense cartoonish blood, intense violence, and mild suggestive themes. You must be logged in to view 13+ projects.
Anything more inappropriate than what a 13+ project would contain is not welcome on Hatch, and you will receive punishment for attempting to upload projects of such nature.
